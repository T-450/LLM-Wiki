In this paper, we propose a high-efficient root cause localization approach for availability issues of microservice systems, called \app.
It dynamically constructs a service call graph and analyzes possible anomaly propagation chains by traversing the graph along anomalous service calls.
Different from existing approaches, \app~uses customized models based on machine learning and statistical methods to detect different types of service anomalies (i.e., performance, reliability, traffic) and employs a pruning strategy to eliminate irrelevant service calls in anomaly propagation chain analysis.
The accuracy and efficiency of \app~have been confirmed by both experimental studies and the practical application in Alibaba.
Future work will be focused on efficiently combing traces, logs, and metrics for more accurate and explainable root cause localization.
